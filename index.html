About Us

THDC Institute of Hydropower Engineering and Technology is a first constituent institute of Uttarakhand Technical University with the aim of becoming a world class comprehensive institute with mission of high-quality undergraduate programs and high caliber research development in cutting-edge areas..The institute believes that learning occurs in both the classroom and the laboratory. Therefore, the curriculum of UG programs(B.Tech in ECE,EE,CSE,ME,Civil) having various practical based courses in every Semester. This allows students to experience first-hand, the joys and challenges of research knowledge. The institute aims to produce undergraduates with a good theoretical and practical foundation in science and engineering backed by excellent research experience who can thrive in a research and innovation intensive environment. What is Our Goal?

The main objective of the conference is to provide the platform to researchers, academicians, research scholars, engineers and students to present their recent research work, and share their experiences, new ideas, in the field of engineering and technology. The conference will provide the opportunity for academicians and researchers from different areas and universities to interact with the delegates of renowned industries and universities so they can enhance their research skill and also collaborate with the best in the business.
